Best Practices

  • Drip Irrigation: Save water by delivering it directly to the roots.
  • Water Timing: Irrigate in early mornings or evenings to reduce evaporation.
  • Monitor Moisture: Use sensors to avoid under or overwatering.
  • Rainwater Harvesting: Collect and store rainwater for sustainable use.
