🐛 Pest Control

Keep your crops safe from pests using natural and effective methods:

  • Use Natural Predators (ladybugs, birds) to control pests.
  • Apply Organic Pesticides like neem oil and garlic spray.
  • Practice Companion Planting (e.g., marigolds repel insects).
  • Inspect crops regularly for early pest detection.
