🐛 Smart Pest Control

Protect your crops naturally with eco-friendly methods.

Best Practices

🐞

Natural Predators

Introduce ladybugs, frogs, and birds to eat pests.

🌿

Organic Pesticides

Use neem oil, garlic, or chili sprays.

🌼

Companion Planting

Grow marigolds and basil to repel harmful insects.

🔍

Regular Inspection

Check leaves and stems for early signs of damage.
